CRP

  CRP (C Reactive Protein) is a serum protein, which is synthesized in the liver. Most normal, healthy human have serum CRP concentration of less than 6 mg/litre. The increase in CRP level level is generally associated with many conditions like rheumatic disease, myocardial infarction or other inflammatory disorder like Alzheimer, Arthritis, Heart disease. It may leads to severe joint pain and suppression of immune system.

Acute increase in CRP level will become negative within 1-2 month and chronic will be within 3 month with all medical parameters.
