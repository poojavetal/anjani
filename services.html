It is mandatory to give first visit for knowing the type of treatment and protocol to be followed by the patient. At this time patient should bring all required reports with him. Further history will be checked out and treatment will be given accordingly.

First Dose
- In first visit treatment will be given to patient that will be considered as first dose which involves medicines of 12 days (Applicable to each type of patient). Afterward there will be gap of 3 days and should take next dose on 15 th day.
Next Dose
- This can be taken either visiting directly to hospital or by online treatment. This type of protocol should be followed till cure of target disease.

Online Treatment
1. Provide new report of 1st dose by mail
2. Provide full address with pin code and contact no.
3. After watching the reports we will call you
4. Do the payment on provided account no.
5. Medicine will be delivered on given address within 3 to 4 working days.
